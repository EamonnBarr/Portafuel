About Us

Welcome to PortaFuel

Portafuel was established in 2018, and our aim is to create a digital fuel station for all motorists.
We provide delivery to the users requested location. Our focus is accessible fuel delivery to a wide range of potential users, without the need to pickup a phone.
Our website and booking system is simple to use, and by coming to your vehicle, we save you from wasting fuel and time. One of our proprietary vans and specialist drivers, safely dispense fuel at a time of your choosing.

It’s time to forget the petrol station, welcome to Portafuel.
